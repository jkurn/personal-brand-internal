Opportunity Gap Analysis

PR & Media Distribution

Most competitors don't include press release distribution or media outreach coordination.

Speaking & Keynote Assets

Limited speaker training; typical gap $4K-5K. Most offer consulting but not production.

Visual Identity Packages

Few integrated visual systems; typical gap $3.5K-5.7K for brand design + photography.

Digital Products

None offer downloadable products/courses for passive income; gap $90-$147 per unit.

Assessment Tools

No pre-built assessment tools or diagnostic frameworks; opportunity for SaaS model.
